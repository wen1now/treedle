Treedle

These are the bare minimum instructions for what to do to play. For an explanation of what's actually going on, click here.

On the right you will be shown a set of past Wordle guess. Initially, there will be nothing there, corresponding to a completely new game of Wordle. Your job is to guess a new word.

Then, the game will randomly generate a new set of guesses and make you try again.

Your aim is to choose the optimal word at each step, where the optimal guess is the word that minimises the average number of guesses you would need to finish the game.
